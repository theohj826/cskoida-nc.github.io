Hay Distributing




Project Completed: 7/28/16


Project Summary

Since 2001, Hay Distributing has been the exclusive retail distributor in Charlotte, NC and Greenville/Spartanburg, SC of the Red Bull Energy Drink. They are located at 11140 Rivers Edge Rd Pineville, NC 28134. This project was one of our first large-scale projects and was completed in 3 phases. The first phase was re-doing the office space with 8, 4' LED Tubes and 53, 2x4 LED Panels. The second phase was the outside and the warehouse of the building with 6 LED wall packs, 8 LED pole lights, and 35 LED high bays. The third phase was miscellaneous fixtures such as LED BR30s and LED Flood Lights. The total project cost was $28,108, but with the Duke Energy Smart $aver incentive it was reduce to only $13,728!
